About

World Space Organisation

The World Space Organisation (WSO) is World’s gateway to space. Its mission is to shape the development of World’s space capability and ensure that investment in space continues to deliver benefits to the citizens of all nations and the world. WSO is an international organisation with 197 countries. By coordinating the financial and intellectual resources of its members, it can undertake programmes and activities far beyond the scope of any single country.

More information

WSO job is to draw up the World space programme and carry it through. WSO'S programmes are designed to find out more about Earth, its immediate space environment, our Solar System and the Universe, as well as to develop satellite-based technologies and services, and to promote World industries. WSO works closely with all space organisations and countries.
